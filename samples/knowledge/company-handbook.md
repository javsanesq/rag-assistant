# Company Handbook

Our customer support team must acknowledge inbound refund requests within 2 business days.

Refunds for annual plans are available within 30 calendar days of purchase when the requester provides the original invoice number.

Security incidents must be escalated to the on-call engineer immediately and recorded in the incident register within 1 hour.
