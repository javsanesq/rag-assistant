# Atlas Legacy Refund Rules 2025

This archived policy is retained for historical audit only and must not be used for current customer decisions.

Annual plan refunds were previously available within 14 calendar days of purchase.

Monthly plan refunds were previously available only when requested on the same calendar day as purchase.

Current refund policy is maintained in the 2026 support refund document.
