# Atlas Support Refund Policy

Annual plan refunds are available within 30 calendar days of purchase when the customer provides the original invoice number.

Monthly plan refunds are available within 7 calendar days if the workspace has fewer than three active users.

Refund requests must be acknowledged within 2 business days.

Enterprise refund exceptions require approval from Finance Operations.
