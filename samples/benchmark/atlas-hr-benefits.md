# Atlas HR Benefits Handbook

Full-time employees receive 16 weeks of paid parental leave after six months of continuous employment.

Remote employees can request a home office reimbursement of up to 750 USD once per calendar year. The reimbursement requires a manager approval note and an itemized receipt.

The wellness stipend is 90 USD per month and can be used for gym memberships, meditation apps, or physical therapy.

Contractors are not eligible for the parental leave benefit or the wellness stipend.
