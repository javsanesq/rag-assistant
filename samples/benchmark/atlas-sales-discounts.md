# Atlas Sales Discount Rules

Account executives may approve discounts up to 12 percent without finance review.

Discounts above 12 percent and up to 25 percent require regional sales director approval.

Discounts above 25 percent require both finance review and VP Sales approval.

The standard nonprofit discount is 20 percent for verified nonprofit organizations.
