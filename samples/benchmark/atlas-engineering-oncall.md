# Atlas Engineering On-Call Guide

Primary on-call engineers must acknowledge production pages within 10 minutes.

If a page is not acknowledged after 10 minutes, the escalation policy notifies the secondary engineer and incident commander.

Post-incident reviews must be drafted within 3 business days after incident closure.

The on-call handoff note must include open alerts, risky deployments, and planned maintenance windows.
