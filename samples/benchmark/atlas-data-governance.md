# Atlas Data Governance Standard

Raw support tickets that contain personal data must be deleted or anonymized within 30 days after ticket closure.

Analytics exports may retain aggregated metrics for 24 months if the export contains no direct identifiers.

Datasets used for model evaluation must record the source system, extraction date, owner, and approved retention period.

No employee may upload customer personal data into an unapproved external AI tool.
