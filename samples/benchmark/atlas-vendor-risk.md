# Atlas Vendor Risk Policy

New vendors that process customer data require a security review before contract signature.

Critical vendors must provide SOC 2 Type II evidence every 12 months.

The vendor risk owner must document subprocessors, data residency, breach notification terms, and exit plan coverage.

Vendors with unresolved high-risk findings cannot be approved for production access.
