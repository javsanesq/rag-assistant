# Orion Product Operations Guide

The Orion dashboard displays 12 active operational panels in the default launch workspace.

The feature flag for the widget recommender is ORION_WIDGET_RECOMMENDER_V2.

Beta customers can enable Orion forecast cards only after their workspace has at least 90 days of activity history.

The product analytics owner for Orion is the Growth Intelligence team.
