# Atlas Security Incident Runbook

SEV-1 security incidents must page the on-call engineer within 5 minutes of confirmation.

The incident commander must open the incident bridge, assign a scribe, and start the incident timeline before customer communications begin.

Customer-facing updates for SEV-1 incidents must be drafted within 20 minutes and approved by legal before publication.

The emergency rotation keyword for identity provider compromise is KESTREL-42-ZETA.
